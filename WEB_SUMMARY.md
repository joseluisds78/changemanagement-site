## Add Your Articles (30 minutes)

### 1. Create Article
Create file: `articles/your-slug.md`

Use format from EXAMPLE_CAB_vs_CCB.md

Example:
```markdown
# Your Article Title

**Published:** January 15, 2024 | **Read time:** 8 minutes

---

## Introduction
...
```

### 2. Register Article
Update `script.js`:

```javascript
{
    id: 'your-slug',
    title: 'Your Title',
    date: '2024-01-15',
    excerpt: 'Brief excerpt',
    readTime: '8 min read'
}
```

### 3. Done
Article automatically appears on website.
